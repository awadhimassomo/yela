Who We Are

Building young leaders who transform communities

Youth Empowerment and Leadership Association (YELA) is a non-governmental organization established in 2023 and officially registered in 2024 under registration number 00NGO/R/7660.

Founded by the young female leader Mwanamvua Nuru, YELA empowers young people to become active contributors to sustainable social and economic development through community-based programs, training, advocacy, and partnerships.

Vision

Empowered youth shaping resilient, inclusive, and sustainable communities.

Mission

To equip young people with the knowledge, skills, opportunities, and leadership needed to drive social innovation, promote equality, protect the environment, and build thriving communities.

Our Commitment

To build a generation of informed, skilled, and responsible leaders who can drive positive change within their communities and beyond.
